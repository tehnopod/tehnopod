About us

The definition for quality is also our vision.

Founded in 1984, Tehnoguma–Nikolov Ltd. – Štip has grown from a family legacy into a leading rubber product manufacturer. Our work supports many industries, with a focus on stable long-term partnerships and consistent quality.

With continuous investment in equipment, expertise and people, we built modern production supported by R&D and a laboratory. We manufacture under ISO 9001 standards (IATF 16949:2016 in progress) with strict quality control and reliable delivery.

TehnoPod is our rubber flooring line — created to bring industrial-grade rubber know-how into gyms, workshops, farms and everyday spaces. All TehnoPod products are produced in-house, with controlled materials and finishing.
