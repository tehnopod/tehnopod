About TehnoPod

Rubber flooring made for daily performance.

Founded in 1984 in Štip, Tehnoguma–Nikolov Ltd. has grown from a family legacy into a trusted rubber manufacturing partner. For decades, we’ve been producing elastomer-based products for many industries — always with the same focus: consistent quality, reliable delivery, and long-term relationships.

With continuous investment in equipment, people, and know-how, we built modern in-house production supported by R&D and a laboratory. Our work follows ISO 9001 quality management (IATF 16949:2016 certification in progress), meaning every batch is controlled for performance, durability, and repeatability.

TehnoPod is our rubber flooring line — created to bring industrial-grade rubber experience into gyms, workshops, farms, playgrounds, and everyday spaces. We don’t outsource production: TehnoPod flooring is made in-house with controlled materials and finishing, so what you order is what you get — same feel, same grip, same performance over time.

We believe flooring should do more than “look good.” It should protect joints, reduce noise, stay stable, and handle daily impact. That’s why our TehnoPod range includes EPDM tiles, rubber rolls, and industrial mats designed for comfort, safety, and long life — indoors and outdoors.

With our principle of caring about customer satisfaction and never compromising on quality, TehnoPod has become one of the recognizable rubber flooring names in North Macedonia and beyond. We’re especially known for our double-layer EPDM speckled tiles, built for high wear resistance, clean aesthetics, and dependable grip — the kind of surface you can train on, work on, and trust.

Why TehnoPod?

Because your floor should work as hard as you do.

At TehnoPod, we don’t see flooring as just rubber on the ground. We see it as the foundation of movement, strength, safety, and everyday performance.

Who is behind TehnoPod?

Built by people who understand rubber. We manufacture everything in-house — real control, real knowledge, and real responsibility. We don’t outsource quality — we create it.

Can it handle impact?

Yes. Dropped weights. Heavy machines. Constant steps. TehnoPod flooring absorbs shock, reduces noise, and protects your base surface — and protects you.

Why double-layer EPDM tiles?

Strength with style. Our speckled EPDM tiles are built for durability, grip, and long-term resistance — indoors and outdoors. Strong enough for performance. Clean enough for modern spaces.

Is installation hard?

No glue. No stress. Most TehnoPod systems are designed for easy placement — just position and use. If your needs change, you can remove or rearrange it.

Do you offer installation service?

Yes. If you want it done for you, our team can install your flooring properly — correct layout, alignment, and finishing for long-term performance.

How about maintenance?

Low maintenance. Long life. Easy to clean, resistant to wear, designed for years of use. It’s not just a purchase — it’s an investment in durability.

Can you make custom solutions?

Yes. Every gym, workshop, farm, or facility is different. Tell us your area, load, and purpose — we’ll help you choose the right thickness, density, and solution.

Why do customers choose you?

We care about satisfaction — not just sales. Quality builds trust, and trust builds long-term partnerships. That’s why TehnoPod is recognized in North Macedonia and abroad.
